INDUSCAPE

Induscape is an urban aural experience triggered by touch, tap, scratch, whistle.

This installation of three sound objects made up of recycled industrial lamp shades is the amplification of retired metal and live modification of its sound by human manipulation. Induscape challenges urban noise, manipulates it and allows humans to create their own aural experience.

This art installation was featured at Inhouse Film Festival in 2014 and Open Warehouse Day in 2015, London.
